Office of the Municipal Planning And Development Officer

Qualifications, Powers and Duties (R.A. 7160)

  • Formulate integrated/comprehensive development plans and other planning documents and policies for consideration of the local government development council;
  • Conduct continuing studies, researches and training programs necessary to evolve plans and programs for implementation;
  • Integrate and coordinate all sector plans and studies undertaken by the different functional groups or agencies;
  • Monitor and evaluate the implementation of the different development programs, projects, and activities in the city in accordance with the approved development plan;
  • Prepare comprehensive plans and other development planning documents for the consideration of the local development council;
  • Analyze the income and expenditure patterns, and formulate and recommend fiscal plans and policies for consideration of the finance committee of the local government unit;
  • Promote people participation in development planning within the local government unit;
  • Exercise supervision and control over the secretariat of the local development council;
  • Exercise such other powers and perform such other duties and functions as maybe prescribed by law or ordinance.
